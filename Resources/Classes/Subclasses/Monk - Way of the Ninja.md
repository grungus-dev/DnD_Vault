**Monks of the Way of the Ninja move unseen through the shadows, striking down the unwary and vanishing again with ease. They blend their training in stealth and assassination with a focused mind and mystical teachings. Their rigorous preparation sharpens their minds and bodies, giving them supernatural abilities of stealth and making them phantoms in the eyes of many.**

## NINJA FEATURES 

| Class Level | Feature                               |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------- |
| 3rd         | Ninjutsu Training, Sudden Strike +1d6 |
| 6th         | Ninjutsu Master, Sudden Strike +2d6   |
| 11th        | Ghost Mind, Sudden Strike +3d6        |
| 17th        | Ghost Walk, Sudden Strike +4d6        |

# [[Ninjutsu Training]]
When you choose this tradition at 3rd level your special training gives you abilities beyond just physical prowess. You gain the following benefits:

**Ghost Step**. As an bonus action, you can spend 1 ki point to become invisible for a number of rounds equal to your proficiency bonus. The effect ends if you attack or cast a spell.

**Tools of Trade.** You gain proficiency with poisoner's kit and thieves tools.

**Physical Expertise.** You gain Expertise in the Acrobatics or Athletics skill Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of the chosen proficiencies.

# [[Sudden strike]]
At 3rd level your strike quickly and with deadly precision. Once per turn, you can deal an extra 1d6 damage to one creature you hit with an attack if you have advantage on the attack roll The attack must use unarmed strike or a monk weapon.

You don't need advantage on the attack roll if another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it, that enemy isn't incapacitated, and you don't have disadvantage on the attack roll

The amount of the extra damage increases as you gain levels in this class, as shown in the Ninja table.

# NINJUTSU MASTER
**Ghost Strike.** At 6th level, you move like a phantom, catching foes that thought themselves safe. You can spend 2 ki point as a bonus action to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space adjacent to a target creature you can see.

**Speed Climb.** At 6th level, you can scramble up or down walls and slopes with great speed You gain a climb speed equal to your move speed but you must end the round on a horizontal surface (such as the ground or a rooftop) If you are not able to end the round on horizontal surface you fall down.

# GHOST MIND
At 11th level your mind is as slippery as your body, granting you the ability to evade and recognize even the slightest threats. You gain the following benefits:

**Undetectable.** You are protected from all devices and spells that detect or read thoughts and your position. You gain advantage on saving throws against effects from Divination school of magic that reveal your position or read your thoughts, like for example Scrying or Detect Thoughts. In the case of scrying that scans an area the creature is in, such as Arcane Eye, the spell works but the creature simply isn't detected

**Sixth Sense.** As an action you can spend 5 ki points to focus your inner eye and reveal what is hidden. You can see in normal and magical darkness, see invisible creatures and objects and see into the Ethereal Plane within 120 feet.

# GHOST WALK
At 17th level you can enter the Ethereal Plane for an extended period of time. You can cast Etherealness once per long rest, but the duration is 1 hour instead of 8 hours.
