*Level 2 Devil Fruit Ability*

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 30 feet  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

A tangle of lion's mane tentacles 30 feet long and 5 feet wide summon from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

(reskin of Aganazzar's Scorcher)
