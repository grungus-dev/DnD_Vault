*2nd level Devil Fruit Spell*

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 50 feet  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

An elongated tentacle . The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Constitution saving throw. On a success, the effect ends on the target.

(reskin of Hold Person)
