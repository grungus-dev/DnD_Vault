When you choose this tradition at 3rd level your special training gives you abilities beyond just physical prowess. You gain the following benefits:

**Ghost Step**. As an bonus action, you can spend 1 ki point to become invisible for a number of rounds equal to your proficiency bonus. The effect ends if you attack or cast a spell.

**Tools of Trade.** You gain proficiency with poisoner's kit and thieves tools.

**Physical Expertise.** You gain Expertise in the Acrobatics or Athletics skill Your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses either of the chosen proficiencies.
